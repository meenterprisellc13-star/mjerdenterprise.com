Fundraising May 2026

M.J.E.R.D Enterprise Launches Wefunder Regulation CF Community Round

M.J.E.R.D Enterprise, Inc. officially launches its community investment round on Wefunder, targeting between $50,000 and $124,000 under Regulation CF. The raise is structured as a Revenue Share Loan offering investors 3% of gross revenue until they receive a 2.2X return (early bird) or 1.9X return (standard). The minimum investment is $100, making this opportunity accessible to everyday investors who want to participate in small business acquisition from the ground up.

Corporate January 2026

M.J.E.R.D Enterprise Completes Delaware C Corporation Conversion

M.J.E.R.D Enterprise, Inc. successfully converts from M.J.E.R.D Enterprise LLC (Florida) to a Delaware C Corporation, establishing the institutional legal foundation required for its multi-vertical acquisition strategy. The company is foreign-qualified in Florida, headquartered in Boynton Beach, and operates under EIN 41-3584270. The M.J.E.R.D Legacy Trust, a South Dakota Revocable Living Trust, is established as the umbrella beneficiary structure across all financial vehicles.

Acquisitions 2025

Ur Gains Fitness Identified as M.J.E.R.D's First Acquisition Target

M.J.E.R.D Enterprise identifies Ur Gains Fitness as its primary first acquisition target in the Fitness & Wellness vertical. The business generated $61,379.77 in documented gross revenue from January through October 2025, demonstrating real operational traction and a loyal customer base in the South Florida market. Capital raised through the current Wefunder campaign will be deployed toward closing this acquisition and establishing M.J.E.R.D's first operational portfolio company.
