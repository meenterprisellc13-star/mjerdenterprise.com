Strategy

Acquisition Criteria

M.J.E.R.D Enterprise applies a consistent, rigorous set of criteria to every acquisition opportunity. We are disciplined buyers who prioritize fundamentals over momentum.

What We Seek

The Qualities We Look For

Every business we evaluate is measured against these eight fundamental qualities.

💰

Positive Cash Flow

The business generates consistent, documented positive cash flow. We do not acquire pre-revenue or speculative ventures. Profitability — actual or clearly recoverable — is non-negotiable.

📈

Healthy Operating Margins

The business operates with margins that support sustainable growth, debt service, and investment without requiring constant capital injection. We look for businesses where financial discipline creates compounding returns.

👥

Loyal Customers

The business has a demonstrated history of repeat customers, strong retention, and community attachment. Customer loyalty is an economic moat that protects revenue regardless of market conditions.

🏭

Established Operations

The business has a proven track record of 3+ years in operation with systems, processes, and infrastructure already in place. We build on foundations, not concepts.

🚀

Growth Opportunities

There are clear, identifiable opportunities to grow revenue, expand margins, or scale operations that have not yet been pursued. We acquire businesses where our operational expertise creates measurable upside.

✍

Ethical Leadership

The business is led by owners who operate with integrity. We conduct thorough due diligence on the ethics, reputation, and character of every seller. The quality of leadership is as important as the quality of financials.

🏗

Strong Fundamentals

Clean books, documented revenue, manageable liabilities, and no hidden obligations. We require full financial transparency as a condition of any acquisition process.

🌟

Long-Term Sustainability

The business serves durable, non-discretionary demand and operates in a market that will exist 20 years from now. We build for legacy, not for the current cycle.
