The M.J.E.R.D Model

How We Create Value

A repeatable four-step process designed to transform undervalued businesses into scalable, cash-flowing portfolio companies.

01
Identify

Source profitable, undervalued businesses in fragmented industries with weak ownership across our four core verticals.

02
Acquire

Negotiate favorable terms using creative structuring — seller notes, earnouts, and strategic debt — to protect capital and align incentives.

03
Optimize

Install financial controls, streamline operations, and introduce automation and KPI dashboards across every acquired business.

04
Scale

Pursue organic growth, expand locations, and execute bolt-on acquisitions within each portfolio company for maximum long-term value.
