The M.J.E.R.D Model

How We Create Value

A repeatable four-step process that transforms undervalued businesses into scalable, cash-flowing portfolio companies.

01
Identify

Source profitable, undervalued businesses in fragmented industries with weak ownership and clear operational improvement opportunities.

02
Acquire

Negotiate disciplined terms using seller notes, earnouts, and creative structuring to preserve capital and align incentives.

03
Optimize

Install financial controls, KPI dashboards, operational automation, and cost discipline across every acquired company.

04
Scale

Drive organic growth, expand locations, execute bolt-on acquisitions, and build sustainable long-term enterprise value.
