M.J.E.R.D Enterprise, Inc. is a diversified holding company built on one principle — ownership creates legacy.

Our Journey

Company Milestones

2024
M.J.E.R.D Enterprise Founded

Martin Dacelin founds M.J.E.R.D Enterprise with a singular vision: to build the Berkshire Hathaway of small business across four core verticals.

2025
Ur Gains Fitness Identified as First Target

M.J.E.R.D identifies Ur Gains Fitness as its primary acquisition target. The business generates $61,379.77 in documented gross revenue from January through October 2025.

January 2026
Delaware C-Corporation Conversion Complete

M.J.E.R.D Enterprise converts to a Delaware C-Corporation (EIN: 41-3584270), establishes the M.J.E.R.D Legacy Trust, and foreign-qualifies in Florida.

May 2026
Wefunder Regulation CF Campaign Launches

M.J.E.R.D launches its community investment round on Wefunder, raising $50,000–$124,000 via Revenue Share Loan to fund its first acquisition.

2026 — Target
First Acquisition Close

Capital raise closes. M.J.E.R.D acquires Ur Gains Fitness, installs financial systems, and begins generating portfolio-level cash flow.
