Why We Exist

Ownership Creates Legacy

M.J.E.R.D Enterprise, Inc. exists because we believe employment builds a career, but ownership builds a legacy. We were founded on the conviction that disciplined acquisition of fundamentally sound businesses — held for the long term — is one of the most reliable paths to generational wealth.

Our ownership philosophy is simple: acquire businesses that deserve to be preserved, invest in the people and systems that make them work, and hold them with the patience that compounding requires.

We are not building a portfolio to flip. We are building a holding company designed to outlast its founder — stewarded with the same discipline whether the markets are favorable or difficult.

Our Journey

Company Milestones

2024
M.J.E.R.D Enterprise Founded

Martin Dacelin founds M.J.E.R.D Enterprise with a singular vision: to build a disciplined acquisition company grounded in long-term ownership and generational wealth.

2025
First Acquisition Target Identified

A fundamentally sound, fitness industry business is identified as M.J.E.R.D’s primary acquisition target, generating $61,379.77 in documented gross revenue from January through October 2025.

January 2026
Delaware C-Corporation Conversion Complete

M.J.E.R.D Enterprise converts to a Delaware C-Corporation (EIN: 41-3584270) and foreign-qualifies in Florida, establishing the institutional foundation for long-term acquisition activity.
