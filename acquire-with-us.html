Work With Us

Acquire With Us.

M.J.E.R.D Enterprise works with business owners, strategic partners, referral sources, capital partners, and future investors. Here is how we can work together.

🏢

Business Owners

If you own a business and are considering a transition, retirement, or sale, M.J.E.R.D Enterprise offers a path forward grounded in respect for what you have built. We are not financial engineers looking to strip value — we are long-term owners looking to preserve and grow what you started.

We work directly with owners to understand your business, your goals, and your timeline before structuring any transaction.

View Acquisition Criteria →
🤝

Strategic Partners

We collaborate with operators, industry specialists, and strategic partners who bring domain expertise to acquisitions. If you have deep experience in a particular industry and are interested in a partnership or co-investment structure, we want to hear from you.

Strategic partnerships allow M.J.E.R.D Enterprise to move with greater confidence and operational depth on every acquisition.

🔗

Referral Sources

Business brokers, M&A advisors, accountants, and attorneys are often the first to know when a business owner is considering an exit. We maintain relationships with referral sources who bring us qualified, well-documented opportunities.

If you regularly encounter business owners considering a sale, we welcome a conversation about how we can work together.

💰

Capital Partners

As M.J.E.R.D Enterprise scales its acquisition pipeline, we evaluate relationships with capital partners — including lenders, family offices, and strategic financial partners — who share our discipline around long-term value creation.

We are selective about capital partnerships and prioritize alignment of incentives and time horizon above all else.
